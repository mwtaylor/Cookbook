Dutch Baby

A fluffy pancake with Seattle origins

This recipe works great to make one individual pancake using a 5 inch cast iron skillet. To make a larger pancake quadruple the recipe and use a 12 inch cast iron skillet.

If a cast iron skillet is not available another similarly sized oven safe pan can be used.

Ingredients

  • Produce
    • Berries for topping
  • Eggs & Dairy
    • 1 egg
    • 1/4 cup almond milk
    • 2 teaspoons unsalted butter
  • Pantry
    • Maple syrup for topping
  • Spices & Seasonings
    • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Staples
    • 40 grams all purpose flour
    • Powdered sugar for topping
    • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  • Prepare
    • Preheat oven to 450 degrees F and place the cast iron skillet in the oven on a middle rack.
  • Make Batter
    • Add the eggs, milk, vanilla, salt, and flour in a food processor or blender.
    • Blend until smooth.
  • Bake
    • Add butter into skillet inside oven and allow to melt.
    • Remove the skillet from the oven and ensure the skillet is evenly coated in melted butter.
    • Gently pour the batter into the skillet and put back into the oven.
    • Bake for about 20 minutes until the top is golden and the pancake is puffed and fluffy.
  • Serve
    • Dust the top with powdered sugar.
    • Serve topped with berries and maple syrup.
