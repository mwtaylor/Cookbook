Banana Split Cocktail

A boozy milkshake with banana and Kahlúa

This recipe is similar to a Dirty Banana like this one from Sugar and Soul

  • Yield: 1 drink
  • Prep time: PT0M
  • Cook time: PT5M

Ingredients

  • Produce
    • 1/2 banana
  • Eggs & Dairy
    • 150 grams strawberry ice cream
  • Pantry
    • Luxardo Maraschino cherries for garnish
    • Chocolate syrup for garnish
  • Alcohol
    • 1 & 1/2 ounce dark rum
    • 1 ounce Kahlúa coffee liqueur
    • 3/4 ounce Giffard Banane du Brésil or similar banana liqueur
  • Staples
    • 1/4 cup ice

Instructions

  • Make the Drink
    • Blend all ingredients except for the garnishes.
    • Serve in a glass decorated with chocolate syrup and garnished with Luxardo cherries and a slice of banana.
