Baked Oats

Breakfast made from oats with the consistency of a cake

Choose between using banana or canned pumpkin, and use only one of chocolate chips or pecans.

  • Yield: 1
  • Prep time: PT5M
  • Cook time: PT30M

Ingredients

  • Produce
    • 1/3 of a banana (if not using pumpkin)
  • Eggs & Dairy
    • 1 egg
    • 2 tablespoons almond milk
  • Pantry
    • 1/2 cup rolled oats
    • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
    • 1/4 cup canned pumpkin (if not using banana)
    • 2 tablespoons chocolate chips (if not using pecans)
    • Pecans (if not using chocolate chips)
    • 2 teaspoons chia seeds (optional)
    • 2 teaspoons sweetened coconut flakes (optional)
  • Staples
    • Cooking spray
    • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
    • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  • Preparation
    • Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
    • Spray an 8 ounce ceramic ramekin with cooking spray.
    • Add the egg, milk, maple syrup, oats, baking powder, and salt into a small blender. Add either banana or pumpkin. Optionally add chia seeds and/or coconut. Blend until smooth.
  • Bake
    • Fill ramekin with batter but don’t overfill, leave a little room to expand. Top with chocolate chips or pecans.
    • Bake for 26 minutes. Cool for 2 to 3 minutes.
