Baked Oats

Breakfast
American
Individual

Breakfast made from oats with the consistency of a cake

Always use all the base ingredients. Then pick one flavor and include those ingredients. Also choose one or more optional mix-ins for additional nutrition. Too many mix-ins can make the ramekin overflow while baking.

  • Yield: 1
  • Prep time: PT5M
  • Cook time: PT30M
Oven Blender

Base Ingredients

  • Eggs & Dairy
    • 1 egg
    • 2 tablespoons whole milk or almond milk
  • Pantry
    • 1/2 cup rolled oats
    • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • Staples
    • Canola oil spray
    • 1/4 teaspoon of baking powder
    • Pinch of salt

Banana Chocolate Chip

  • Produce
    • 1/3 of a banana
  • Pantry
    • Chocolate chips for topping

Pumpkin Pecan

  • Pantry
    • 1/4 cup canned pumpkin purée
    • Pecans for topping

Optional Mix-ins

  • Pantry
    • 2 teaspoons of chia seeds
    • 2 teaspoons of sweetened coconut flakes

Preparation

    • Preheat oven to 350°F.
    • Spray an 8 ounce ceramic ramekin with cooking spray.
    • Add ingredients into a small blender. Blend until smooth.

Bake

    • Fill ramekin with batter but don’t overfill, leave a little room to expand. Add topping.
    • Bake for 28 to 32 minutes. Internal temperature should reach 200°F. Cool for 2 to 3 minutes.
