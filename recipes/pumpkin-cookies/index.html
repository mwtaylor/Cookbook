Chewy Spiced Pumpkin Cookies

Recipe is from In Bloom Bakery

The recipe makes about 20 cookies using a 2 tablespoon cookie scoop. The recipe recommends cooking 6 cookies at a time. The cookie balls can be made slightly larger for 18 cookies by cooking 3 batches of six.

I have found 8 cookies can fit on a sheet. Make two full batches of exactly 2 tablespoon cookies. Then any remaining dough can be baked in a small third batch to get the full 20 cookies.
