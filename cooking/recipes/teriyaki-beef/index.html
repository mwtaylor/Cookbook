Preparation

Marinate the Beef

  • 1 pound of thinly sliced beef
  • 2 tablespoons of Bachan's Sauce

Marinate the beef in 2 tablespoons of Bachan’s sauce for at least 15 minutes.

Preparation

Cook Rice

  • White rice

Cook rice in an rice cooker or Instant Pot and keep warm until ready to serve.

Preparation

Prepare Vegetables

  • 1/2 white onion
  • 1 green pepper
  • Bunch of green onions

Slice the onion. Cut the green pepper into 1/2 inch squares. Thinly slice the green onions.

Preparation

Make the Sauce

  • 4 tablespoons of Bachan's Sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of water
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch

Whisk together 4 tablespoons Bachan’s Sauce and water with cornstarch.

Cook

Heat Pan

  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil

Heat the oil in a pan over medium high heat. Be sure to use a large enough pan that will allow the water from the meat to quickly boil off.

Cook

Sear the Beef

Add marinated beef and cook until seared. Remove from pan and set aside keeping oil and drippings in pan.

Cook

Cook Vegetables

Add green peppers and sliced onion. Stir fry until vegetables are charred and cooked.

Cook

Simmer Sauce

Add sauce and about half of the green onions. Add meat back into the pan. Lower heat and simmer until thickened.

Cook

Toast Sesame Seeds

  • Sesame seeds

Add sesame seeds to another pan and cook over medium heat until toasted.

Serve

Serve

Serve over rice and top with fresh green onion and toasted sesame seeds.
