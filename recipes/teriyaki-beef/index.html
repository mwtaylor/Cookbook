Teriyaki Beef

Simple & Quick Teriyaki Beef

This recipe comes from https://bachans.com/blogs/recipes/classic-seattle-teriyaki-beef

  • Yield: 4 servings
  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 20 minutes
  • Cook Now

Ingredients

  • Meat
    • 1 pound of thinly sliced beef
  • Produce
    • 1/2 white onion
    • 1 green pepper
    • Bunch of green onions
  • Pantry
    • 6 tablespoons of Bachan's Original Japanese Barbecue Sauce
    • 2 teaspoons of water
    • White rice
  • Spices & Seasonings
    • Sesame seeds
  • Staples
    • 2 teaspoons of cornstarch
    • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil

Instructions

  • Preparation

    • Marinate the Beef

      Marinate the beef in 2 tablespoons of Bachan’s sauce for at least 15 minutes.

    • Cook Rice

      Cook rice in an instant pot or rice cooker and keep warm until ready to serve.

    • Prepare Vegetables

      Slice the onion. Cut the green pepper into 1/2 inch squares. Thinly slice the green onions.

    • Make the Sauce

      Whisk together 4 tablespoons Bachan’s Sauce and water with cornstarch.
  • Cook

    • Heat Pan

      Heat the oil in a pan over medium high heat.

    • Sear the Beef

      Add marinated beef and cook until seared.

    • Cook Vegetables

      Add green peppers and sliced onion and cook for two to three minutes.

    • Simmer Sauce

      Add sauce and about half of the green onions. Lower heat and simmer until thickened.

    • Toast Sesame Seeds

      Add sesame seeds to another pan and cook over medium heat until toasted.
  • Serve

    • Serve

      Serve over rice and top with fresh green onion and toasted sesame seeds.
